.. math::
   \phi = \frac{1}{2\pi}\left(\frac{X}{\sqrt{1+X^2}}\tan^{-1}{\left(\frac{Y}{\sqrt{1+X^2}} \right )}+\frac{Y}{\sqrt{1+Y^2}}\tan^{-1}{\left(\frac{X}{\sqrt{1+Y^2}} \right )}\right)

Where :math:`X=\frac{W}{S}` and :math:`Y=\frac{H}{S}`.
